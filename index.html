About

We are a platform that matches real estate investors with willing sellers of low cost, income generating real estate properties in densely populated outskirt areas of Nairobi such as umoja, kayole, kasarani, githurai, kangemi, uthiru, juja that are in delapidated condition and can be improved to increase their earning potential

BUYER

For individuals/companies looking to acquire a viable property as an investment.

  • We notify you of available properties for sale
  • We value the property based on current income and future potential earnings
  • we handle documentation and due diligence
  • Facilitate payments and transfer of property
  • We manage the property on your behalf
SELLER

For property owners looking to offset a property, we match you with buyers/Investors.

  • We list your property to buyers/investors
  • We provide you with a valuation depending on the current income generated
  • we handle documentation and due diligence
  • We facilitate payments and transfer of property

Our process

Sellers

We identify willing sellers of commercial low cost properties in Kenya and we place a bid based on our calculated valuation

Due diligence

Upon agreement of the price, we proceed to carry out due diligence of the property and the documentation provided

Buyers

We match the sellers with buyers and investors willing to put up funds to purchase the property. Money is put in an escrow service until the client has approved the purchase

Deal

Once everything has checked out well, we facilitate transfer of the property and disbursement of payments through the platform and close the deal
